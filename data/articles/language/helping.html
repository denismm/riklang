Helping verbs

Here's some structures that involve verbs collecting other verbs or states of being.

Want:
to want to do (Patient).
I want to sleep.
Me-R-Agent-0
Sleep-V-Patient-0
Want-V-End-2

Child:
to begin to do (Patient).
I start eating.
Me-R-Agent-0
Mouth-V-Patient-0
Child-V-End-2

Learn:
to learn or teach how to do (Patient).
I learn how to work.
Me-R-Destination-0
Work-V-Patient-0
Learn-V-End-2

Have:
to be able to do (Patient).
I can fight.
Me-R-Agent-0
Fight-V-Patient-0
Have-V-End-2

Dead:
to stop doing (Patient).
I stop being happy.
Me-R-Agent-0
Happy-M-Patient-0
Dead-V-End-2

Give:
to be allowed to do (Patient).
The leader allows me to create.
Leader-R-Agent-0
Me-R-Destination
Fruit-V-Patient-0
Give-V-End-3
