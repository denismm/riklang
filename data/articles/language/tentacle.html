Tentacles

The rikchik language is signed by the rikchiks using their seven "speaking tentacles", which are located directly below the eye. Even in the written form, the lines representing tentacle positions are referred to as "tentacles". Each written tentacle represents, the position, shape, and orientation of a speaking tentacle as it is used in the expression of a word. The seven tentacles in a word are the four central morpheme tentacles, the lower left aspect tentacle, the upper relation tentacle, and the lower right collector tentacle.

Throughout this language reference, the aspect, relation, and collector tentacles are colored red, blue, and green respectively. These colors are provided as an aid to learning and are not part of the usual presentation of the language.
