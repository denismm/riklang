Home Rikchik Ground Want Above

Each morpheme consists of four tentacles. A morpheme represents a general concept such as "rikchik", "home", "want", or "above".

Each morpheme falls into a paradigm which serves as a guideline to the meaning of each of its aspects, and of the roles of the relations it collects.

For more information about a particular morpheme, see the dictionary of morphemes.
