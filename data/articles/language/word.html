Rikchik-R-Agent-2

A rikchik word consists of seven tentacles, arranged in four groups:

  • The central morpheme carries the basic meaning of the word.
  • The left-side aspect further refines that meaning.
  • The top-side relation states how the word relates to the rest of the sentence.
  • The right-side collector provides the sentence structure.
The ascii form consists of the codes for the Morpheme, Aspect, Relation, and Collector in that order, separated by hyphens. This is known as MARC order. (Some old pages may use MRAC order.)
