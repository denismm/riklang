Compound Words

Tasty Year Teacher

The Name ("N") aspect is used for both names and compound words. For compound words, every word collected by the base word takes the N aspect, indicating that it's to be considered a more direct part of that word than usual. The other parts of the word function normally.

Usually, any further collections of the compound word are collected by the base word, but in certain cases the collected words collect other words. The most common such case is in telling time.
