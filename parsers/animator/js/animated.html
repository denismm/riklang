This page simulates how Rikchik text looks when actually signed by a rikchik. Each word is rested on briefly and then the tentacles transition to the next word.

Rikchiks have seven pointed "talking" tentacles that shade from white at the tips to a medium green at the base. These form a contrast with the consistently thick green "walking" tentacles behind them.
